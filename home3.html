What We Don't Say

I lost a friend today, though not to death

no final breath, no flowers laid.

We’re both still here, just far apart,

a quiet space that boundaries made.

~~~

No shouting fight, no shattered trust,

just softer lines we drew instead.

A word unsaid, a call not placed,

a silence where we used to thread.

~~~

We laughed like wind through open trees,

we spoke like songs without a key.

But life grew thick with complicated steps,

and fences rose between you and me.

~~~

I miss the ease, the thoughtless days,

the way we'd talk until the night.

Now even kindness feels too close,

and reaching out no longer feels right.

~~~

So here we are - not enemies

just strangers trained to stay away.

I lost a friend today, not out of hate

but simply choosing a different way.
