Common questions

How much does a cleaning cost? +
Pricing depends on your home size and the type of cleaning. Most recurring cleans for a 3BR/2BA home in Greenville run between $150–$220. Deep cleans are typically $250–$400. Request a quote and we'll give you an exact number — no obligation.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning? +
Nope! Most clients aren't home. We'll arrange secure access — lockbox, door code, or hidden key — and lock up when we're done.
Are your cleaners insured? +
Yes — every team member is background-checked, trained, and we carry full liability insurance. Your home and belongings are protected.
What if I'm not happy with the cleaning? +
We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If something isn't right, let us know within 24 hours and we'll come back and re-clean the area — free of charge.
