The Process

Simple to order.
Impossible to forget.

You bring the sound. We turn it into something you'll keep forever.

01

Send Your Sound

After ordering, text or email us any audio — an old voicemail, a phrase you speak into your phone, a baby's heartbeat, a wedding vow, a dog's bark. If it makes a sound, it can be art.

02

We Build the Wave

We convert your audio into a unique soundwave and engrave it onto your chosen piece with precision and care. Every wave is completely one-of-a-kind — because every voice is.

03

Add a QR Code

Optionally, we can add a small QR code to your piece. Scan it with any smartphone and it plays the real audio that made the soundwave. Art that speaks — literally.

04

Ships Free to You

Every single order ships free, anywhere in the USA. Your piece arrives ready to gift or display — already full of meaning, no assembly required.

Meet the Makers

Joel & Cindy Pendl

We're a husband and wife team from Indiana. We started The Soundwave Booth because we believe the most precious things in life are the words we say to the people we love — and we wanted to find a way to make those words last.

It started simply: turning the sounds that matter most into something you can see and hold. A grandmother's voicemail. A baby's first heartbeat. The words spoken on a wedding day.

"The phrase beneath each image was spoken to create the soundwave above it. It's the audio, made visible."

Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know.

Before you order, here are the answers to what we hear most.

What kind of audio can I use?
Almost anything — an old voicemail, a phrase you speak into your phone, a baby's heartbeat, a wedding vow, a dog's bark, a Bible verse you recorded. If it makes a sound, we can turn it into art.
How do I send you my audio?
After ordering, just text or email your audio directly to us — 574-935-3995 or thesoundwavebooth@gmail.com. We accept most formats straight from your phone.
What is the QR code option?
We add a small QR code to your piece. When anyone scans it with their phone, it plays the actual audio that created the soundwave. The art literally speaks.
Ready-made or custom — what's the difference?
Pre-made pieces feature faith-filled, family, and inspirational phrases and ship right away. Custom orders are made from your personal audio — just send it to us after ordering.
How much does everything cost?
Pieces start at $16 for keychains and go up to around $30 for frames and cutting boards. Every single order ships free — no minimum, no exceptions, ever.
What if my audio is old or low quality?
We work with all kinds of audio — old voicemails, scratchy recordings, video audio. We do everything we can to create the most beautiful soundwave possible from whatever you have.
How long does a custom order take?
Custom orders typically take 3–7 business days. Need it by a birthday, anniversary, or holiday? Just let us know and we'll do everything we can to make it happen.
Do you ship everywhere in the USA?
Yes — free shipping to all 50 states. International orders may be available too, just reach out and ask about your location.
