Meet the Makers

Joel & Cindy Pendl

We're a husband and wife team from Indiana. We started The Soundwave Booth because we believe the most precious things in life are the words we say to the people we love - and we wanted to find a way to make those words last.

You'll find us at local markets and events, set up with our booth and our microphone, ready to turn your voice into something you can take home the same day.

"Every piece we make is filled with faith, family, and love. No negativity - just the sounds worth keeping forever."

For custom pieces, order right from our website and we'll ship free to your door. For something made in person, come find us at an event.

Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know.

Can I get a custom piece online?
Yes! You can now record your own voice or upload any audio file directly on our website. We'll craft your custom piece and ship it free within 2 business days of payment processing.
How does the in-person booth work?
Just walk up, speak a phrase into our microphone, and we create a one-of-a-kind piece right there. You'll have your finished soundwave art in hand the same day. It's that simple.
What is the QR code option?
We can add a small QR code to your piece. Scan it with any smartphone and it plays the actual audio that created the soundwave. The art literally speaks.
Do your pre-made pieces ship fast?
Yes - pre-made items are ready to ship and typically go out within 1–3 business days. Every order ships free anywhere in the USA, no exceptions.
How much does everything cost?
Our pieces start at $16 for baby beanies and go up to around $30 for frames and magnets. Every single order ships free - no minimums, no surprise fees.
Where do you set up your booth?
We travel to local markets, craft fairs, and events across Indiana and surrounding areas. Follow us on Facebook or reach out to find out where we'll be next - or to invite us to your event.
What phrases are on your pre-made pieces?
Our pre-made collection features faith-filled phrases, family expressions, and inspirational words - things like "I love you forever," "faith over fear," and "home is wherever you are."
What occasions make the best gifts?
Mother's Day, Father's Day, Christmas, anniversaries, weddings, baby showers, memorials, and birthdays. Honestly - there's no wrong time to give someone something this meaningful.
