Everything You Need to Know

By submitting a rental request, you agree to these terms.

🕐
Pickup & Return Times
  • Pickup available Monday–Friday after 6:00 PM
  • Pickup available Saturdays & Sundays all day
  • Return must happen within the same time windows
  • Exact time coordinated via text after booking confirmed
  • Late returns may incur an additional day charge
🧹
Cleaning Requirements
  • All games must be returned clean and dry
  • Wipe down all surfaces before return
  • Remove any mud, grass, or debris
  • Bags & equipment must be returned in original bags
  • A $15 cleaning fee applies if returned dirty
⚠️
Damage & Replacement
  • Renter is responsible for all damage during rental period
  • Lost or destroyed items charged at full replacement cost
  • Damage assessed at return — no exceptions
  • Replacement costs listed in rental contract signed at pickup
  • Normal wear and tear is expected and accepted
💰
Payment
  • Payment due at or before pickup
  • Accepted: Venmo, cash
  • Rental is not confirmed until payment received
  • No refunds for cancellations within 24 hours of pickup
  • Rescheduling allowed with 48+ hours notice
📋
Rental Contract
  • A rental agreement is signed at time of pickup
  • Contract covers damage, cleaning, and replacement terms
  • Both parties receive a copy at signing
  • Renter must be 18 years or older
  • Renter assumes all liability during rental period
🎮
General Rules
  • Games are for outdoor use unless noted
  • Do not leave equipment in rain or standing water
  • Keep all components together — report missing pieces immediately
  • Owner reserves the right to refuse rental to anyone
  • Contact us anytime — we want your event to be a success!
